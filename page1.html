Meet the Founders

We founded **The Fidget Shop** because we personally understood the power of focused movement. In a world that constantly demands attention and stillness, we recognized that many people—ourselves included—find clarity, reduce anxiety, and improve concentration through simple, tactile interaction. Our goal was to curate a collection of high-quality fidgets that not only serve a purpose but look and feel incredible, making focus accessible and stylish for everyone, from students to professionals.

Rory

Co-founder & Chief Curator

Rory, a design enthusiast, brings a meticulous eye for aesthetics and engineering to The Fidget Shop. Previously working in product development, Rory focuses on sourcing and designing our most unique and quiet fidgets, ensuring every piece meets a high standard for tactile feedback and durability. Rory believes that the perfect fidget should blend seamlessly into your daily life.

Haydan

Co-founder & Community Lead

Haydan's passion lies in the intersection of well-being and technology. Having struggled with focus in professional settings, Haydan is dedicated to building a supportive community around the benefits of fidgeting. Haydan manages customer outreach, educational content, and specializes in selecting our 'Squishy' and stress-relief categories, focusing on sensory stimulation.
