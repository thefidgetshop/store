Meet the Founders

We founded The Fidget Shop because we personally understood the power of focused movement. In a world that constantly demands attention and stillness, we recognized that many people—ourselves included— find clarity, reduce anxiety, and improve concentration through simple, tactile interaction. Our goal was to curate a collection of high-quality fidgets that not only serve a purpose but look and feel incredible, making focus accessible and stylish for everyone—from students to professionals.

Rory — Co-founder & Chief Curator

Rory, a design enthusiast, brings a meticulous eye for aesthetics and engineering to The Fidget Shop. With a background in product development, Rory focuses on sourcing and designing our most unique and quiet fidgets, ensuring every piece meets high standards for tactile feedback and durability.

Haydan — Co-founder & Community Lead

Haydan’s passion lies in the intersection of well-being and technology. Having struggled with focus in professional settings, Haydan is dedicated to building a supportive community around the benefits of fidgeting and manages customer outreach, educational content, and product curation in our “Squishy” and sensory relief collections.
